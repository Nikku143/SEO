.Define Favicon and give an illustrative.example.?
ANS:-

It is a shortened expression for 'favorite icon. ' 
It helps you identify the visited website among the many open tabs.
 For example, The fancy P that appears on the tab left of the ProCreator website's address bar is our favicon.
 People tend to confuse it with the logo
